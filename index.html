You will be given a random number at the start of each game. When you click on a crystal you will add a certain amount of points to your total score. The value of each crystal displays when clicked.

In order to win the game your total score must match the random number but if your total score is above the random number you lose!

Your choices are four of the rarest crystals on the planet; Larimar, Black Opal, Tanzanite and Red Beryl. Good luck mining!!!
